About TipToe Soles

TipToe Soles is a shoe company based in Texas USA. We manufacture high quality shoes which we provide to other companies to re-sell. Recently we have decided to launch our own range of everyday, comfort shoes. Providing affordable, high quality shoes for everyone.


We will be expanding locations to Australia soon.
