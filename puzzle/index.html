How to play:

  • 1. Choose a board size
  • 2. Upload an image, move it within the red box to finalise your puzzle
  • 3. Slide the tiles by clicking a tile adjacent to the blank tile (W,S,A,D keys also work)
  • 4. Recreate your original image with the blank tile at the bottom right of the board
  • 5. Try get the fastest time you can, good luck!
